About Legacy Sport Solutions PCC Limited

Legacy Sport Solutions PCC Limited is structured as a Protected Cell Company (PCC) focused on investment and equity acquisitions in sports and adjacent sectors. The PCC structure enables defined initiatives to be structured through dedicated cells, with clear governance and documentation.

Who we are
  • ✓
    Investment-only mandate We focus exclusively on equity acquisitions, structured investments, and co-investments — no representation services.
  • ✓
    Disciplined underwriting Clear downside controls, governance standards, and investor-grade reporting expectations.
  • ✓
    Long-term value creation Active support on strategy, KPI cadence, and performance improvement frameworks.
Why a PCC structure
  • ✓
    Defined initiatives Specific opportunities (e.g., club investments, sports platforms, infrastructure) can be organized under dedicated cells.
  • ✓
    Clarity for partners Participation can be aligned to a specific mandate with transparent documentation and reporting.
  • ✓
    Risk management principles Ring-fencing principles support separation between initiatives where implemented and documented under applicable law.

Note: Any investment or participation is subject to applicable law, due diligence, and definitive documentation.
