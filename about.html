About Legacy Sport Solutions PCC Limited

About Legacy Sport Solutions PCC Limited

Legacy Sport Solutions PCC Limited is structured as a Protected Cell Company (PCC) focused on investment and equity acquisitions in sports and adjacent sectors. The PCC structure enables defined initiatives to be structured through dedicated cells, with clear governance and documentation.

Who we are
  • ✓
    Investment-only mandateWe focus exclusively on equity acquisitions, structured investments, and co-investments — no representation services.
  • ✓
    Disciplined underwritingClear downside controls, governance standards, and investor-grade reporting expectations.
  • ✓
    Long-term value creationActive support on strategy, KPI cadence, and performance improvement frameworks.
Why a PCC structure
  • ✓
    Defined initiativesSpecific opportunities can be organized under dedicated cells (subject to applicable law and documentation).
  • ✓
    Clarity for partnersParticipation can be aligned to a specific mandate with transparent documentation and reporting.
  • ✓
    Risk management principlesRing-fencing principles support separation between initiatives where implemented and documented.

Note: Any investment or participation is subject to applicable law, due diligence, and definitive documentation.
