About Legacy Sport Solutions PCC Limited

Legacy Sport Solutions PCC Limited is a specialist sports investment and management platform structured as a Protected Cell Company (PCC). This structure enables the creation of separate protected cells dedicated to specific sports projects, player development programs, club investments, and strategic partnerships.

Why a PCC structure?

A PCC allows defined initiatives to be ring-fenced within individual cells. Each cell may be set up for a specific project, mandate, or investor group, supporting clear governance, transparency, and risk management.

  • Segregated assets and liabilities: each cell can be structured so that assets and liabilities are separated from other cells.
  • Project clarity: investors and partners can participate in specific initiatives with defined scope.
  • Operational efficiency: central governance and administration can sit at the core, supporting multiple initiatives.

What we do

Legacy Sport Solutions PCC Limited supports sports stakeholders through strategic advisory, project structuring, and the development of investable opportunities across the football ecosystem.

  • Club investment and strategic partnership initiatives
  • Youth development programs and elite pathways
  • Sports infrastructure and academies
  • Commercial strategy, sponsorship, and distribution support

Important note

Certain projects or investment opportunities may be conducted through individual protected cells of Legacy Sport Solutions PCC Limited. Details of any specific cell arrangement, where applicable, will be provided in the relevant project documentation.
