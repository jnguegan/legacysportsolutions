About

Legacy Sport Solutions PCC Limited is structured as a Protected Cell Company (PCC) focused on investment and equity acquisitions in sports and adjacent sectors. The PCC structure enables dedicated cells to support specific initiatives with clear governance and ring-fencing principles.

Who we are

Legacy Sport Solutions PCC Limited identifies, evaluates, and executes equity investments and strategic acquisitions with disciplined underwriting, robust reporting standards, and a long-term value creation approach. We operate strictly within an investment mandate — no player/coach representation.

Why a PCC structure

A Protected Cell Company (PCC) structure can support ring-fenced initiatives under one corporate umbrella. Subject to applicable law and documentation, a PCC may establish separate protected cells for specific projects, mandates, or investor groups.

  • ✓
    Cell-based structuring Dedicated cells may be created for defined opportunities (e.g., club investments, sports platforms, infrastructure).
  • ✓
    Clarity for partners Participation can be aligned to a specific initiative with clear scope and documentation.
  • ✓
    Risk management Ring-fencing principles support separation between initiatives, where implemented and documented.

Note: The exact legal effect of segregation depends on applicable law, the PCC’s constitutional documents, and the terms of each transaction and cell arrangement.
